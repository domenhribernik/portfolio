The Drawing Room

The parlour where everybody draws at once. Open a room, hand round the code, and when the bell rings, every guest sketches on the same sheet.

How the evening goes
  1. I.The host opens a room and shares its four-letter code.
  2. II.Guests gather in the lobby; everyone gets an ink of their own.
  3. III.The host rings the bell, and one shared sheet opens for all.
No accounts, no gallery: rooms are anonymous and swept away after twelve quiet hours.

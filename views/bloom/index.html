fair questions

Asked before, answered honestly.

Why would I pay for a web page?

You are not paying for a page, you are paying for the moment: the right date, their name on the card, and a bouquet that is still there on your tenth anniversary. A real bouquet costs ten times more and is compost by Friday. If the moment does not land, the guarantee above applies.

Is it really forever?

Every paid bouquet stays live for as long as this site exists, and every one comes with a printable botanical postcard, so even if the internet ends you keep the flowers. I will never quietly delete a paid bouquet.

When does the shop open?

The builder is live today, free. Paid bouquets and Petal Post are being grown now; the founding list gets first access and keeps the 4 EUR/mo price for life. One email when it opens, nothing else.

What happens if I cancel Petal Post?

Scheduled deliveries stop. Every bouquet you already sent stays in bloom. Nothing you gave someone is ever taken back.

Can I gift a subscription?

That is planned for launch: a year of Petal Post as a gift code. Tell me in the note below if you would want that, it moves it up the list.

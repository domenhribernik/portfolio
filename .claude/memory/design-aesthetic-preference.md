Domen is a developer and also a self-described creative/artist, and wants his portfolio to feel like that. For the homepage + About redesign (2026-06) he rejected a dark "brutal black terminal + acid green" direction as "not who I am" and "scary," and chose a light **Editorial Gallery** look: gallery cream (#f6f2ea), warm ink text, a single clay/vermilion accent (#d4451f) with sparing pine, Fraunces serif display + IBM Plex Sans body, generous whitespace, hairline rules, numbered section eyebrows.

**Why:** he cares that the site looks great to real visitors and reflects a warm, human, creative personality, not a cold/techy cliche. He also explicitly wanted away from the default "AI purple" (#667eea/#764ba2).

**How to apply:** for his design work, lean warm/light/refined/editorial over dark/techy by default; offer bold-but-tasteful directions and let him pick; avoid generic AI aesthetics (purple-on-dark, Inter). See the implemented theme tokens in `body.editorial` in [base-style.css]. Related: [[homepage-redesign-architecture]].
